Hi there! I am a PhD student in the [Department of Philosophy](https://philosophy.utoronto.ca/), and a collaborative program student in [Mark S. Bonham Centre for Sexual Diversity Studies](https://sds.utoronto.ca/), both at the University of Toronto.

I am interested in philosophical inquiries into spatiotemporality, trauma, care, and life and death, drawing on the resources of 20th-century continental philosophy, psychoanalysis, East Asian literature and philosophy, and trans and queer philosophy. I am also interested in cybernetics and the relations between technology and humanity.

Outside of philosophy, I am also a trans community supporter and member of [One Among Us](https://oneamongus.ca). I use the name [Beiyan Shu](https://sauri.ca) for some community activities and writings.

I live with my partner, [oxalica (she/her)](https://github.com/oxalica). She is a freelance software developer and an active contributor to the Rust/Nix communities. Check her GitHub profile for some fantastic projects!
